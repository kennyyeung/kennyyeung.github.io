Designing a course on design

Giving an introduction on design to peers

Since in the Fall semester of 2017, I have instructed the Human-Centered Design decal course (DesInv 98) at UC Berkeley.


On average there are about 80 students each semester. We meet for two hours once a week.


From giving lectures, leading workshops, and guiding students through hands-on projects, I was able to mold the curriculum to something more valuable and interactive for students.


Check out the course site here!


Feel free to message me for more details or if you'd like to talk about the curriculum! The course is still changing, being improved upon each semester, so any feedback or suggestion for the course is welcome.
